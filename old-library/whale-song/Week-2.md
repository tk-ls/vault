## one
dear journal,
today at Bridgewood with Wendell was both amazing and awful. I learned that asl has this incredible history, how it came from France and evolved into something new. it made me think maybe blue 55 and I could understand each other somehow.
but then the junior high kids started signing so fast I couldn't keep up. when Wendell joked about signing "like an old person" for me, it really hurt. not because he does it, but because he has to. I'm so tired of being treated like I'm less than everyone else.
iris
