## Positive Integers
- **Definition:** Whole numbers greater than zero (1, 2, 3, ...).
- **Operations:** Know addition, subtraction, multiplication, division. Use PEMDAS/BODMAS for order.
- **Prime Numbers:** Only divisible by 1 and itself (e.g., 2, 3, 5).
- **Composite Numbers:** More than two factors (e.g., 4, 6, 8).
- **Factors:** Numbers that divide evenly into a given number.
- **Multiples:** Results of multiplying a number by whole numbers.
- **HCF:** Greatest common factor shared between numbers.
- **LCM:** Smallest shared multiple between numbers.
- **Divisibility Rules:** Know shortcuts for 2, 3, 4, 5, 6, 8, 9, 10.
- **Advanced:** Prime factorization, Euclidean algorithm.
## Negative Integers
- **Definition:** Whole numbers less than zero (-1, -2, -3, ...).
- **Number Line:** Visual tool for comparing values.
- **Addition/Subtraction:** Use number line rules.
- **Multiplication/Division Rules:**
	- Positive × Positive = Positive
	- Positive × Negative = Negative
	- Negative × Positive = Negative
	- Negative × Negative = Positive
- **Absolute Value:** Always positive distance from zero.
- **Order of Operations:** PEMDAS still applies.
- **Directed Numbers:** Used in contexts like temperature or depth.
- **Advanced:** Large/small negatives in word problems.
## Decimals
- **Definition:** Numbers with a decimal point.
- **Place Value:** Tenths, hundredths, thousandths, etc.
- **Compare:** Start from the left, digit by digit.
- **Rounding:** 5 and up rounds up, otherwise down.
- **Add/Subtract:** Line up decimal points.
- **Multiply:** Ignore decimals, multiply, then place decimal.
- **Divide:** Shift decimal in divisor and dividend.
- **Fraction ↔ Decimal:** Divide or express over power of 10.
- **Recurring Decimals:** Have repeating digits.
- **Advanced:** Scientific notation, significant figures.
## Fractions
- **Definition:** Part of a whole (numerator/denominator).
- **Equivalent:** Different forms, same value.
- **Simplify:** Divide by HCF.
- **Improper:** Numerator ≥ denominator.
- **Mixed Numbers:** Whole + fraction.
- **Convert:** Between improper and mixed using division.
- **Add/Subtract:** Use common denominator.
- **Multiply:** Top × top, bottom × bottom.
- **Divide:** Flip second fraction, then multiply.
- **Reciprocal:** Swap numerator and denominator.
- **Advanced:** Complex fractions, algebraic applications.
## Percentages
- **Definition:** Out of 100 (%).
- **Conversions:**
	- Fraction to %: Multiply by 100
	- Decimal to %: Multiply by 100
	- % to Fraction: Put over 100, simplify
	- % to Decimal: Divide by 100
- **Finding % of a Number:** Multiply by decimal/fraction form.
- **Increase/Decrease:** Change expressed as % of original.
- **% Change Formula:** (New - Original) / Original × 100
- **Advanced:** Compound interest, tax, profit/loss.
## Algebraic Techniques
- **Pronumerals:** Letters representing unknowns.
- **Terms:** Parts of expressions split by +/−.
- **Coefficients:** Numbers before pronumerals.
- **Constants:** Numbers without variables.
- **Like Terms:** Same variable, same power.
- **Collect Like Terms:** Add/subtract coefficients.
- **Expanding:** Use distributive property.
- **Simplifying:** Expand + combine like terms.
- **Substitution:** Replace variables with values.
- **Writing Expressions:** Translate word problems.
- **Advanced:** Factorizing, solving equations.
## Number Properties & Patterns
- **Commutative:** a + b = b + a; a × b = b × a
- **Associative:** (a + b) + c = a + (b + c); same with ×
- **Distributive:** a(b + c) = ab + ac
- **Identity:** a + 0 = a; a × 1 = a
- **Inverse:** a + (−a) = 0; a × 1/a = 1
- **Number Patterns:** Recognize and extend numeric rules.
- **Spatial Patterns:** Patterns in shape sequences.
- **Sequences:**
	- Arithmetic: Constant difference
	- Geometric: Constant ratio
- **Advanced:** Fibonacci, special sequences, generating formulas.
