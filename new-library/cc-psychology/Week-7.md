## 21. Rorshach and Freudians
1. Rorshach would show a patient a series of ink blots and record what they saw to determine how people projected their personal associations onto random shapes. ✓
2. Personality is defined as your distinctive and enduring characteristic patterns of thinking, feeling, and behaving. ✓
